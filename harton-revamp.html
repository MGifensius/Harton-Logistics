OUR JOURNEY

Company Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, discover how we've grown to become your trusted logistics partner

2015
PT Harton Transportasi Internasional was founded with a clear mission: to help businesses and individuals overcome the complexities of permissions management and customs clearance. We recognized a critical gap in the market and set out to fill it with expertise and dedication.
2017
Responding to growing market demand, we strategically expanded our service portfolio to include stevedoring and trucking operations. This expansion marked our evolution from a specialized service provider to a comprehensive logistics solution partner.
Today
We now offer a complete suite of integrated logistics services, from permissions handling and customs clearance to stevedoring, trucking, and warehousing. Our end-to-end solutions empower businesses to focus on growth while we handle the complexities of logistics.
